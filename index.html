About the Liberation War Archive

The Liberation War Archive is a comprehensive online repository dedicated to preserving the history and memories of Bangladesh's 1971 Liberation War. Our mission is to document, digitize, and make accessible the historical materials related to this pivotal moment in Bangladesh's history.
